The Panther Lounge

Welcome to the Panther Lounge.

The Panther Lounge is a recreational space owned by Florida Tech's Student Government Association and co-run by the Florida Institute of Technology Science Fiction and Fantasy Club (FITSSFF) and Anime Club. With some exeptions, all Florida Tech students are free to use the lounge every day from noon to ten pm.
